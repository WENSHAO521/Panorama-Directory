Academic Profile

Interdisciplinary Academic Inquiry

Chengwen Song is a dedicated researcher affiliated with Kookmin University, specializing in personnel and organizational administration. His interdisciplinary work spans across public service motivation, the cognitive-psychological impacts of artificial intelligence, and digital transformation in business environments.

Through rigorous empirical and theoretical inquiry, his publications contribute to a broader understanding of how modern governance, societal domains, and individual cognition interact within contemporary institutional frameworks. His research frequently appears in interdisciplinary journals spanning humanities, social cognition, and administration.
