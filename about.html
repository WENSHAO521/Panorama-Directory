About Us

Panorama Scholarly Group

Headquarters: Berlin, Germany

Model: Diamond Open Access

Panorama Scholarly Group (PSG) is an independent academic publisher based in Berlin, Germany. We are committed to dismantling academic barriers and facilitating the free flow of interdisciplinary knowledge through rigorous double-blind peer review and the Diamond Open Access publishing model.

Our diverse portfolio of academic journals—including the exclusively English-language Global Review of Humanities, Arts, and Society (GRHAS), alongside Health Nexus, SILENCE, and Journal of Social Cognition and Communication—focuses on frontier explorations in humanities, social cognition, public administration, interdisciplinary medical research, and art theory.

This directory showcases the global network of principal investigators and editorial board members who form the bedrock of our academic integrity. PSG firmly believes that transparent academic governance, stringent research ethics, and a diverse international perspective are crucial to addressing the complex challenges of modern society.
