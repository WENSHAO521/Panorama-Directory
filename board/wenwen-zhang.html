Editorial Board Member

Wenwen Zhang

Ningbo University

Dr. Wenwen Zhang is a Lecturer in the Faculty of Foreign Languages at Ningbo University. She holds a Ph.D. in English from Peking University, providing a rigorous humanistic foundation for her interdisciplinary scholarship.

Her primary research lies at the intersection of utopian fiction and artificial intelligence. Deeply engaged in the evolving dialogue between the humanities and technological advancement, she brings a unique humanistic perspective to the analysis of technocriticism in contemporary British and American fiction. Her scholarly work has been published in esteemed CSSCI-indexed journals, including Contemporary Foreign Literature.

As a recognized scholar in literary and cultural studies, she serves as the principal investigator for a prominent provincial-level research project funded by the Zhejiang Provincial Philosophy and Social Sciences Foundation. Furthermore, she actively contributes to scholarly publishing as an Editorial Board Member for AI & Future Society, where she addresses the complex ethical and societal implications of emerging technologies.
