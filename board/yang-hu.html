Official Academic Profile

Yang Hu (胡扬)

Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM)

Yang Hu is a dedicated researcher specializing in the digital transformation of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and organizational management. She is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Management at Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM).

Her interdisciplinary research spans artificial intelligence (AI) ethics, business management strategy, international education, and human resource development. She possesses a robust academic foundation with prior degrees in Public Administration (M.A., Yunnan Normal University) and Computer Science and Technology (B.S., Huanghe Science and Technology University).

In addition to her research outputs, Yang Hu actively contributes to the academic community as a verified peer reviewer for leading international journals, including Humanities & Social Sciences Communications (Springer Nature) and SAGE Open.
