Assistant Editor

Xingkai Zhang

Xinjiang Medical University

Xingkai Zhang is a Master's candidate in Clinical Medicine at Xinjiang Medical University, specializing in Laboratory Medicine and Diagnostics. He earned his Bachelor's degree in Medicine from Southwest Medical University.

His primary research focuses on the bioinformatics analysis of metabolites and their relationship with various diseases. He is particularly dedicated to investigating the physiological and pathological impacts of high-altitude environments on the human body, frequently utilizing advanced statistical approaches such as Mendelian randomization and genetic correlation analyses.

He serves a vital role in scholarly publishing as an Assistant Editor for both Silence and Health Nexus, facilitating rigorous academic communication in the medical and life sciences.
