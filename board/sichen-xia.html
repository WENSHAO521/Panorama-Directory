Editorial Board Member

Sichen Xia

Nanjing Medical University

Sichen Xia is a medical researcher in Stomatology at Nanjing Medical University, China, currently undertaking his clinical training at the Affiliated Stomatological Hospital.

His primary research focuses on Oral Epidemiology, encompassing biostatistics, clinical research, preventive dentistry, and population health. His academic work explores the critical intersection of clinical patient care and epidemiological studies, with a firm dedication to advancing dental public health and improving health outcomes on a broader population level. His rigorous methodological approach is evident in his wide array of publications addressing complex health behaviors, mental health factors, and frailty networks.

Contributing to the advancement of scholarly publishing, he currently serves as an Editorial Board Member for the academic journal Silence.
