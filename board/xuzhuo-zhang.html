Xuzhuo Zhang

Universiti Malaya

Xuzhuo Zhang is a researcher in Computer Science at Universiti Malaya, currently pursuing his Ph.D. He possesses a strong academic foundation in cybersecurity, holding a Master of Cyber Security from the University of Adelaide and a Bachelor of Information Technology from the University of South Australia.

His primary research focuses on digital forensics and the maintenance of chain of custody within ambient social media environments through federated learning. His technical investigations aim to resolve data distribution distortion in deep learning frameworks via geometric rectification of imbalanced datasets. Furthermore, his work extends to constructing traceability-responsibility convergence models to address ethical parameters and credibility within automated AI investigation processes.

He serves on the Editorial Board of AI & Future Society, contributing his expertise in digital forensics, deep learning frameworks, and AI ethics to the scholarly community.
