Editorial Board Member

Qiong Xie

Gannan Normal University

Qiong Xie is a faculty member specializing in Finance at the School of Economics and Management, Gannan Normal University. Her primary research focuses on sustainable development management, corporate finance, securities investment, and financial derivatives.

Dedicated to bridging theoretical constructs with practical industry applications, her research explores the synergistic effects of organizational innovation and knowledge mechanisms within the context of sustainable development, particularly among small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).

In addition to her research, she is deeply committed to international finance education. She continuously optimizes curriculum systems and teaching methodologies for both undergraduate and graduate student cohorts, aiming to cultivate interdisciplinary financial talents equipped with a global perspective and robust practical capabilities. She also serves as an Editorial Board Member for AI & Future Society.
