Yongxin Chen

Civil Aviation Flight University of China

Dr. Yongxin Chen is a Lecturer at the School of Economics and Management, Civil Aviation Flight University of China. He holds a Ph.D. in Business Management from Queen’s University Belfast and a First-Class Honours Master’s degree in Financial Economics from Cardiff University, bringing a rigorous international perspective to his academic endeavors.

His primary research interests lie at the intersection of corporate digital transformation, innovation management, and sustainable development. His scholarly contributions have been recognized and published in world-leading international journals, including Technovation, the Journal of Business Research, and Business Strategy and the Environment.

Beyond his research, Dr. Chen maintains strong ties with both academia and industry. He serves as an Editorial Board Member for AI & Future Society and is an active peer reviewer for journals affiliated with the International Information Management Association. Furthermore, he holds positions as a Research Fellow at the Digital Economy Center of the Sanya Economic Research Institute, an expert for the Sichuan Internet of Things Industry Development Alliance, and a key contributor to the SME Case Center of Chongqing College of Mobile Communication.
