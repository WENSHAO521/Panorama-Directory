Assistant Editor & Reviewer

Zhili Zhu

Xiamen University

Zhili Zhu is a postgraduate researcher in Marxist Philosophy at Xiamen University, studying under the supervision of Professor Lin Mi. His academic foundation is deeply rooted in the study of Marxist Basic Principles and Foreign Marxism.

His primary research focuses on Marxist spatial theory, urban Marxism, contemporary radical left-wing thought and its critiques, and the critique of biopolitics. He has established a strong presence in the academic discourse, notably serving as the sole representative from Western China at the 3rd National Undergraduate Forum on Marxist Theory. Furthermore, he has actively participated in and delivered presentations at over ten prominent academic conferences.

In the realm of academic publishing, he currently serves as an Assistant Editor and Peer Reviewer for the Global Review of Humanities, Arts, and Society.
